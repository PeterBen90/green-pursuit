About Green Pursuit

At Green Pursuit, we understand that a company's auto fleet is more than just a means of transportation—it's the calling card of the company. A clean, well-maintained fleet not only reflects professionalism and attention to detail but also acts as valuable advertising.

Every vehicle on the road represents your brand, making cleanliness and sustainability essential to creating a positive impression and reinforcing your company’s commitment to excellence. We have cleaned and maintained thousands of fleet vehicles across all of Ontario. We're a fully mobile cleaning service, we come to you!

Our
Services

Green Pursuit offers a wide range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of your fleet,
specializing in exterior washes, interior cleaning with Steam Cleaning and Decontamination solutions, including:

Exterior Cleaning

Our exterior cleaning services keep your vehicles, and your company's image, looking spotless and well-maintained.

Interior Cleaning

We pay attention to the details, ensuring the interior of your fleet is safe, clean and comfortable.

Pressure Washing

Get rid of tough stains, dirt, and grime with our powerful pressure washing services.

Decontamination

We specialize in creating custom decontamination solutions for our clients job and workplace related exposures that comply with Occupational Health and Safety policies for safe and clean workspaces.

Green Cleaning Products

We use eco-friendly cleaning practices and products to minimize our impact on the environment.
