About Green Pursuit

At Green Pursuit, we are passionate about keeping your Auto Fleet clean and sustainable. Our journey began with a mission to provide eco-friendly, efficient, and top-quality mobile cleaning solutions. With a team of dedicated professionals, we ensure your fleet looks its best inside and out, while minimizing our environmental impact. We have cleaned and maintained thousands of Fleet vehicles across all of Ontario. We come to you!
